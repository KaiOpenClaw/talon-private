## Production Guidelines

### Visual Brand Standards
- **Color Palette:** Dark theme with purple/blue accents
- **Typography:** Modern, readable fonts
- **Logo Placement:** Subtle but visible
- **Consistent UI:** Professional, clean interface

### Audio Considerations
- **Background Music:** Upbeat but not distracting
- **Sound Effects:** Minimal, professional
- **Narration:** Optional for some platforms
- **Accessibility:** Captions for all text

### Engagement Optimization
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds grab attention
- **Value:** Show clear benefit/problem solving
- **Action:** Clear next step (star, deploy, visit)
- **Hashtags:** Relevant, not overwhelming

### Platform-Specific Adaptations

#### Twitter/X
- Square format preferred
- Auto-play without sound
- Captions essential
- Hashtag optimization

#### LinkedIn
- Professional tone
- Business value focus
- Horizontal format works
- Longer captions welcome

#### Instagram Stories
- Vertical format required
- Interactive elements (polls, questions)
- Bright, engaging visuals
- Story highlights for persistence

#### TikTok/Instagram Reels
- Vertical format
- Trendy music/sounds
- Fast-paced editing
- Hashtag challenges

## Content Calendar Integration

### Posting Strategy
- **Monday:** Feature highlights (Clips 2, 5)
- **Wednesday:** Before/after comparisons (Clip 1)
- **Friday:** Behind-the-scenes/automation (Clip 4)
- **Weekend:** Mobile/accessibility focus (Clip 6)

### Cross-Platform Timing
- **LinkedIn:** 8-10 AM business hours
- **Twitter:** 12-3 PM peak engagement
- **Instagram:** 6-9 PM evening engagement
- **TikTok:** 6-10 PM prime time

### Analytics Tracking
- View completion rates by platform
- Engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments)
- Click-through rates to GitHub
- Conversion to stars/deployments

## Technical Specifications

### Recording Setup
- **Screen Resolution:** Platform-specific
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS (60 FPS for smooth motion)
- **Audio Quality:** 48kHz, noise-free
- **Lighting:** Consistent, professional

### Export Settings
- **Codec:** H.264 for compatibility
- **Bitrate:** Optimized for platform limits
- **Color Space:** sRGB for web
- **Metadata:** Include relevant tags

### File Management
- **Naming:** `social-{platform}-{feature}-{date}.mp4`
- **Storage:** `/content/marketing/social-clips/`
- **Backups:** Version control for assets
- **Templates:** Reusable graphics and animations
