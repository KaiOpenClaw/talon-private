## Technical Production Notes

### Voiceover Specifications
**Style**: Professional, confident, technical but accessible
**Tone**: Problem-solving, empowering, not overly salesy
**Pace**: Clear enunciation, strategic pauses for visual beats
**Gender**: Male or female, age 25-40, native English speaker
**Quality**: Studio-quality recording, no background noise

### Visual Treatment
**Theme**: Professional dark mode throughout
**Animation**: Subtle, purposeful movements (no flashy effects)
**Typography**: Clean, readable fonts (Inter/SF Pro)
**Color Scheme**: Consistent with brand (terminal green, blue accents)
**Resolution**: 1920x1080, 30fps for smooth playback

### Music & Audio
**Background Music**: 
- Subtle, modern tech/productivity track
- Low volume, non-intrusive
- Builds slightly toward end
- Royalty-free or original composition

**Sound Effects**:
- Minimal, tasteful UI sounds
- Click/transition sounds for navigation
- No overwhelming or distracting audio

### Text Overlays & Graphics
**Style**: Clean, minimal, high contrast
**Animation**: Fade in/out, subtle slide effects
**Timing**: Synchronized with voiceover beats
**Positioning**: Strategic placement avoiding UI obstruction
